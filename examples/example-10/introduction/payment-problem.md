# Cross-Chain Payment Gateway: When Money Needs to Travel Between Blockchains

You’re running an online store. Customer wants to pay with tokens on Avalanche. You want to receive USDC on Base.

Traditional approach: tell the customer “sorry, we only accept payments on Base.” They either bridge manually (annoying, slow, high fees) or they don’t buy.

You lose sales because of chain fragmentation.

Or maybe you accept multi-chain payments but manually handle each one. Customer pays AVAX on Avalanche. You monitor. Once confirmed, you manually bridge. Then you convert to USDC. Then you receive it on Base.

Three manual steps. Fifteen minutes. Multiple gas fees. Room for human error.

There has to be a better way.

What if customers could pay on any chain, and you automatically receive the right token on your preferred chain? No manual intervention. No bridging headaches. Just seamless cross-chain commerce.

That’s what we’re building.
